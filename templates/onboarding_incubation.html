About Voice Incubation

The Voice Incubation mode is designed to facilitate the creation of a new, distinctive tone of voice and brand voice for entities based on inputs from multiple stakeholders. It provides a structured framework for synthesizing diverse perspectives into a cohesive, authentic voice that reflects the entity's values, purpose, and desired positioning.

This process ensures the new voice maintains internal consistency and external differentiation, creating a unique identity that resonates with your target audience while remaining true to your organization's core values and mission.

What You'll Need

  • Entity foundation information (name, purpose, industry, target audience)
  • Core values from multiple stakeholders
  • Desired psychological positioning
  • Archetypal preferences
  • Linguistic style preferences
  • Emotional expression parameters

What You'll Get

  • A comprehensive voice profile for your new entity
  • Detailed voice characteristics and guidelines
  • Sample content demonstrating the new voice
  • Implementation recommendations for different content types
  • Voice consistency evaluation criteria
  • Adaptation guidelines for different contexts
